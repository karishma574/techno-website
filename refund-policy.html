No Refund Policy

At techno Designs, all sales are final. We operate under a strict no-refund policy for all our services and digital products. Once a purchase is completed and payment is processed, refunds will not be provided under any circumstances.

Why We Have This Policy

Our services and digital products provide immediate access and are non-returnable. Due to the nature of our offerings:

  • Services are delivered instantly upon purchase
  • Digital content cannot be "returned" once accessed
  • Our processing resources are consumed immediately
  • Custom services begin automatically after payment
  • Credits for services are allocated immediately to your account

Before You Purchase

We strongly encourage you to:

  • Review our service features and limitations carefully
  • Watch our demonstration videos to understand the product
  • Contact our support team with any questions before purchasing
  • Start with our lower-tier packages to test our services
  • Read our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy

Technical Issues

If you experience technical difficulties with our service, our support team will work diligently to resolve any issues. However, technical problems do not qualify for refunds. We will:

  • Provide technical support to resolve issues
  • Offer alternative solutions when possible
  • Extend service access if downtime affects your usage

Fraudulent or Unauthorized Charges

If you believe a charge was made in error or without your authorization, please contact us immediately. We will investigate such claims, but this does not guarantee a refund. You may also need to dispute the charge with your credit card company or bank.

Service Modifications

We reserve the right to modify, suspend, or discontinue our services at any time. In such cases, we will provide reasonable notice, but refunds will not be issued for any unused credits or purchased packages.

Legal Compliance

This no-refund policy is enforced to the fullest extent permitted by law. Some jurisdictions may not allow certain limitations on refunds, so some of these restrictions may not apply to you. However, where legally permissible, this policy takes precedence.

Contact Information

If you have questions about this refund policy, please contact us.
